BrightTV: Viewership Analytics & Growth Strategy

1. Overview

BrightTV is a streaming platform focused on rapidly growing its subscriber base. This project analyzes large-scale user profile and session-level data to uncover viewing patterns, engagement drivers, and churn risks. The goal is to equip the Customer Value Management (CVM) team with actionable insights to improve engagement and drive growth.

2. Methodology & Key Insights

Approach

Data Integration: Merged user profile data with session-level viewing records.
Big Data Processing: Used Databricks to clean, transform, and process millions of records.
Timezone Normalization: Converted UTC timestamps to South African Standard Time (SAST) to reflect local viewing behavior accurately.
EDA: Performed segmentation across geography, age, gender, and content categories.

Key Insights

Monday Slump: Mondays show the lowest engagement, presenting an opportunity for targeted content drops and campaigns.
Gauteng Dominance: Gauteng drives nearly double the watch time compared to other provinces.
Gender Gap: An 8:1 male-to-female viewing ratio highlights a strong opportunity for female-focused content.
Live Sports Impact: Over 45% of total consumption is driven by live sports events, confirming strong demand for real-time content.

3. Executive Summary

BrightTV’s growth is currently driven by a loyal male, sports-focused audience concentrated in urban regions. However, significant expansion opportunities exist through demographic diversification. Targeting underrepresented audiences and addressing low-engagement periods (like Mondays) through CVM campaigns can significantly boost platform growth and retention.
